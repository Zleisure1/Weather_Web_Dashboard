Latitude vs. Humidity

Plot of latitude vs. humidity.

This chart shows the distribution of humidity over various latitude bins. In general we see high humidity at 0-9 degrees latitude, low humidity at 20-29 degrees latitude, and fairly consistent humidity levels from 30 to 79 degrees latitude, but at lower humidity levels than the 0-9 degree latitude bin. This is as expected as rainforests are concentrated around the equator so we see the highest average humidity at the 0 to 9 degree latitude bin. Deserts are concentrated around 30 degrees latitude and have low humidity so we see the lowest average humidity bin at the 20 to 29 degree latitude bin.
